* VISA APPLICATION DENIAL
In the event of a denial by the Indonesian Government, the Government Visa Tax (PNBP) is strictly non-refundable. However, our Administration Fee is 90% refundable, and 10% will be counted as a non-refundable administrative charge.

* GOVERNMENT TAX (PNBP)
Once the Government Visa Tax (PNBP) is paid to the authorities or if the application is rejected/applied, this portion is 100% non-refundable.

* FORCE MAJEURE
In the event of unforeseen circumstances beyond our control, such as natural disasters, we reserve the right to modify or suspend our refund policy. Administration fees remain subject to the standard 10% non-refundable deduction.

* APPROVED E-VISA
Once your e-VISA has been approved, all fees (Tax & Admin) are non-refundable as the service has been fully delivered.

* REFUND METHOD
We can only refund to the account your money was sent from. Refunds by credit card will be processed via bank transfer directly to your bank account.

* CANCELLATION AND REFUND
If you cancel your application and request a refund before completion, a 90% refund of the Administration Fee applies. A 10% administrative charge is retained for work already performed.

* OFFSHORE & ONSHORE APPLICATIONS
a. A 10% administrative deduction applies to all refund requests for the service portion.
b. Refunds made by bank transfer will have the bank fee deducted from the final amount (Client bears transfer costs).
c. The rate refunded will follow the Indonesian bank currency rate at the time of processing.

* REFUND TIMELINE
Your refund will be processed within 7-14 business days from your formal request via email to support@indonesianvisas.com
