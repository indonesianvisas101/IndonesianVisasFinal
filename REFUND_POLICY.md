* CLOSURE OF BORDERS We strive to follow the regulations set by the Indonesian Government. If the regulation changes and it affects the validity of your e-VISA, such as the closure of borders, and if your e-VISA was approved and expires, we are unable to provide a refund.
* APPROVED E-VISA Once your e-VISA has been approved, we cannot provide a refund.
* APPLICATION SENT FOR PROCESSING If we have sent your application to Immigration for processing and have paid the billing, we cannot provide a refund.
* REFUND METHOD We can only refund to the account your money was sent from.
* CANCELLATION AND REFUND If you cancel your application and request a refund, a 10% administration fee applies.
